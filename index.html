About game

what is our product

Souleater is hack&slash rogue-like game about viking who found the cursed SoulEater and now has to kill enemies to feed his weapon, otherwise axe will drain health from him. Maps are procedurally generated among villages, buffs and more.

Features

Our game offers

    • Fighting with time

    The axe is constantly feeding, either on you or the enemies.

    • Fast-paced combat

    Roll through your enemies and dish out quick attacks

    • Clan auras

    Every opposing clan has a totem with special aura, boosting enemy capabilities.

    • Procedurally generated environment

    With randomly generated islands and village auras, each gameplay session is unique.
